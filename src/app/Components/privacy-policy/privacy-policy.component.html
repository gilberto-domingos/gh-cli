Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 31, 2025

AI-Text-Expander is a Chrome extension developed by Gilberto Domingos and designed to enhance productivity by expanding user-defined shortcuts into full text.

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personally identifiable information is collected (e.g. name, email, age, etc.)
  • No health, financial, or authentication data is collected.
  • No geolocation or IP address is tracked.
  • No browsing history, communication content, or keystrokes are stored or sent to servers.

Local Storage

All shortcut data is stored locally on the user's device using chrome.storage.local. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your computer.
  • No external servers are involved.
  • Only you have access to your data.

Permissions

The extension only interacts with text fields (input, textarea, contenteditable) to replace user-defined shortcuts. It does not modify any other parts of the visited websites.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or external scripts.
